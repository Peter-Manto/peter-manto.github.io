About Me

I am a second year Echols Scholar and College Science Scholar at the University of Virginia studying in Physics and Economics and a minor in Computer Science.

Research

I worked with the spin physics group led by Gordon Cates at the University of Virginia. Our group worked in a collaboration with Jefferson National Laboratory to probe the structure of the neutron through spin-exchange optical pumping (SEOP). I assisted with producing, preparing, and testing the helium cells used as electron scattering targets at Jefferson Lab.
